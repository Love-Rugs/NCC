We are starting our spring development block from **Monday, 23 February 2026**.

## Key updates for parents

- Training times remain unchanged for all current squads.
- Please ensure players bring:
  - football boots
  - shin guards
  - a full water bottle
- Arrive **10 minutes early** for register and warm-up.

Focus this month is first touch under pressure and quick passing patterns.

### 2018s
Defending as a unit and transition play will be introduced in small-sided games.

### 2019s
Ball mastery and movement fundamentals continue with game-based sessions.

### 2020s
Confidence and coordination remain the main priority through fun skill stations.
